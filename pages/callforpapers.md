The E3Vision-2026 is a platform both for industry and academia to present and discuss the innovations, trends, and concerns as well as the challenges encountered and solutions. We invite researchers, professionals, and industry experts to to submit their original, unpublished research contributions to these tracks that are not currently under consideration by another conference/journal, and be part of a collaborative effort to shape a sustainable and eco-conscious energy future.

**Track 1: Advanced Power Systems, Grid Stability & High-Voltage Engineering**
- Real-Time Grid Monitoring, Protection & Wide-Area Measurement Systems (WAMS)
- High-Voltage DC (HVDC), FACTS & Flexible Transmission Technologies
- Resilient Power System Planning under Extreme Events & Cyber-Physical Threats
- AI-Based Load Forecasting, Grid Optimization & Fault Diagnosis
- Advanced Insulation Materials, High-Voltage Testing & Electromagnetic Transients

**Track 2: Renewable Energy Conversion, Control & Net-Zero Technologies**
- Solar, Wind & Hybrid Renewable Energy Conversion Systems
- Grid-Forming Inverters & Advanced Control for Variable Renewables
- Green Hydrogen, Fuel Cells & Power-to-X Technologies for Net-Zero
- Sustainable Microgrids, Community Grids & Rural Energy Solutions
- Lifecycle Assessment, Carbon Accounting & Climate-Neutral Energy Strategies

**Track 3: Power Electronics & Digital Grid Platforms for Next-Gen Energy Systems**
- Wide-Bandgap Devices (SiC/GaN) & High-Efficiency Converter Topologies
- Digital Twins, AI/ML & Virtualized Control for Smart Grids
- Smart Inverters, Solid-State Transformers & AC/DC Hybrid Grids
- Cybersecurity, IoT & Edge Computing for Digital Energy Management
- Power Quality Enhancement & Advanced Harmonic Mitigation Techniques

**Track 4: Battery Technologies, Energy Storage & Supercapacitors**
- Next-Gen Li-ion, Solid-State & Beyond-Li Battery Chemistries
- Battery Management Systems (BMS), Diagnostics & State-Estimation
- Supercapacitors, Hybrid Storage & Fast-Charging Technologies
- Grid-Scale Storage, Long-Duration Energy Storage (LDES) & Thermal Storage
- Recycling, Second-Life Batteries & Sustainable Manufacturing

**Track 5: Electric Mobility, Autonomous EVs & Smart Energy Management**
- EV Powertrains, Motor Drives & High-Efficiency Propulsion Systems
- Autonomous Driving, V2X Communication & Intelligent Transport Systems
- Ultra-Fast Charging, Wireless Charging & Bidirectional Charging (V2G/V2H)
- EV Integration with Smart Grids, Microgrids & Renewable Energy
- Fleet Electrification, e-Mobility Infrastructure Planning & Policy Innovation

Papers undergo a single-blind review process, by experts drawn from reputed organizations, across the world. Nontrivial, unpublished and original contributions will only be considered for the conference. The conference will have main papers, presentations, and poster presentations for short papers.
